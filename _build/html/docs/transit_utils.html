transit_utils.transit_utils.transit_duration(params, which_duration='full')[source]¶

Calculates transit duration

Args:
params: dict of floats/numpy arrays, including
params[“per”] - orbital period (any units) params[“p”] - ratio of the planet to the star’s radius params[“b”] - impact parameter in units of stellar radius params[“a”] - semi-major axis in units of stellar radius
which_duration (str):

“full” - time from first to fourth contact “center” - time from contact to contact between planet’s center and

stellar limb

“short” - time from second to third contact

Raises:
ValueError: If which_duration is not ‘full’, ‘center’, or ‘short’.
Returns:
transit_duration: transit duration in same units as period
